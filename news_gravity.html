How to Play

Overview

A twist on tic-tac-toe where gravity shifts the board between stages. Two players (O and X) compete over 4 stages to score the most points.

Stages

  • There are 4 placement stages (S0–S3). Each stage, O places first, then X.
  • After each stage, gravity is applied in a direction (shown in the sequence bar), sliding all pieces that way.

Scoring

  • After the 3rd gravity: if a player has a 3-in-a-row, they earn 3 points.
  • After the 4th gravity: if a player has a 3-in-a-row, they earn 2 points.
  • A player can score at both gravity stages (3 + 2 = 5 possible).
  • If both players have a line at the same gravity stage, neither scores for that stage.

Final Tile

  • After all 4 stages, one cell remains empty.
  • The game checks if placing each player's symbol there would complete a line.
  • Each player who would complete a line earns 1 point.

Winning

The player with the higher total score wins!

Match Formats

  • 1 Round — a single game.
  • 2 Rounds — players swap O/X between rounds.
  • 4 Rounds — rounds 1–2 use the original gravity; rounds 3–4 use reversed gravity with swapped starting roles, so both players get to go first on each gravity configuration.
