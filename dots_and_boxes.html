How to Play

Setup

A grid of dots is created (from 4×4 up to 10×10). Two players take turns.

Taking Turns

  • On your turn, click any available line between two adjacent dots to draw it.
  • Lines can be horizontal or vertical.

Claiming Boxes

  • If your line completes the 4th side of a box, you claim that box — it gets marked with your initial.
  • You can claim multiple boxes with a single line if it completes more than one.
  • After claiming a box, you must take another turn immediately.

Winning

  • The game ends when all possible lines have been drawn.
  • The player who claimed the most boxes wins!
