How to Play

How to Play

Objective

Guess the hidden word one letter at a time (or guess the full word) before the hangman drawing is complete. You have 6 wrong guesses before it's game over.

Scoring (1 Player & Co-op)

  • Correct letter: +1 point per occurrence in the word.
  • Completing a word: +5 bonus.
  • Failing (hangman complete): −3 penalty.
  • In multi-round games, the goal is to finish with a positive score.

1 Player

A random common word is chosen. Click letters on the keyboard or type the full word to guess. Wrong letters and wrong word guesses each add a body part.

2 Players — Competitive

  • Both players submit N words for the other to guess.
  • Words are shuffled and each player guesses through them.
  • Points per word: (6 − wrong guesses) × 10 if solved, 0 if the hangman is completed.
  • You can see the opponent's hangman progress and score in real time.

2 Players — Co-op

  • A common random word is chosen for each round.
  • Each turn, both players simultaneously guess a letter or word.
  • If both guess the same wrong letter/word, only 1 body part is added instead of 2.
