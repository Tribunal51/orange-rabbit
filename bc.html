How to Play

How to Play

Objective

Guess the secret word in as few attempts as possible.

Feedback

  • Bull — a letter in your guess is in the word and at the correct position.
  • Cow — a letter in your guess is in the word but at the wrong position.

Only the count of bulls and cows is shown, not which letters they refer to.

1 Player

The game picks a random word. Keep guessing until you find it.

2 Players Online

  • 1 Round: Both players guess the same randomly chosen word. Fewer guesses wins.
  • 2 Rounds: Round 1 is a common word. In Round 2, each player sets a word for the other to guess. Total guesses across both rounds determines the winner.
